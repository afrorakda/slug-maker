# slug-maker

Part of the Fridge Combo project. A stoic URL converter that strips away the noise to create clean, SEO-friendly slugs.

## 1 Tool / 1 Action

Just paste your title or text. **Slug Maker** instantly purges all non-alphanumeric characters (including Japanese and special symbols) and converts your input into a lowercase, hyphen-separated string. No settings, no clutter—just pure, valid URLs.

## Stripped & Sharp

- **Stoic Filtering:** Strictly extracts `a-z`, `0-9`, and `-`. Everything else is gone.
- **Smart Formatting:** Automatically merges multiple spaces/hyphens and trims the ends.
- **Length Monitor:** A subtle badge warns you if your slug exceeds 75 characters—the SEO "sweet spot."
- **Pulp Inspiration:** Inspired by the clean, bold utility of a classic "Banana Slug."

Perfect for developers, bloggers, and minimalist creators who need a "clean-as-hell" permalink in a single click.
